Be.Artsy.Designs was established in 2020 and began as a small company selling only canvas art. Since the coompany has expanded its inventory to include wearable art and a variety of artistic services. The company is owned by Britney Diggs, who is also the artist behind the inventory. Britney found her love for art and drawing as a young child. Britney worked as an art teacher for a local company named Access Art for a many of years. And recieved a Bachelors degree in Art Education/ Digital Media Communications from The University Of Maryland Global Campus.
